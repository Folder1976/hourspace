concept

hOUR SPACE is the first time club in Tel Aviv. Here you can have some tea 

or coffee with your friends, play board games, work or study, listen to a lecture or 

participate in a workshop. It’s a free space and it’s up to you how to use it. 

Our main feature is you pay only for the time you spent here.


First of all we are a social space,
and one of our main goals is to provide a place where
people can meet self-actualize, express themselves
and feel at home.


Are you a freelancer or a student?
Sometimes it’s difficult not to be distracted at home, in coffee shops they don’t
seem to be happy to have you there and most of co-workings look really solemn.
Me made our best to create a space nice, quite and all prepared for productive
work and study.

Do you like having guests but your living room is too small?
Now you can invite everyone to hOUR SPACE!
We have enough space for each person to feel comfortable. …


You are really welcome here!
High-speed Internet, nice and calm atmosphere, free access to the printer and scanner
and unlimited tea and coffee are waiting for you here!


Do you have a low battery or you don’t know where to print a boarding pass?
Are you cold or hot or tired? Have you lost your way?
Come to hOUR SPACE! Here you’ll find a charger, a printer, some hot and cold
beverages, help with directions, good recommendations and friendly smiles:)
